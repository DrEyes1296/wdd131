Website Plan: DaMoSa Cleaning Services

Site Name

DaMoSa Cleaning Services

This name was chosen because it is the official name of the family-owned business. It is professional, direct, and clearly identifies the company and its primary function.

Site Purpose

The website's purpose is to establish a professional online presence for Damosa Cleaning Services. It will serve as a primary marketing tool to attract new commercial clients in the North Shore and Boston, MA area. The site will clearly detail the specialized services offered (commercial cleaning, floor care, carpet care, post-construction) and provide a straightforward way for potential clients to schedule a free, on-site estimate, thereby generating qualified leads for the business.

Scenarios

  1. An office manager is looking for a new cleaning crew that can work after business hours. They want to know: "Can I schedule a cleaning service that won't disrupt my employees during the workday?"
  2. A construction contractor has just finished a major renovation and needs the site cleaned thoroughly before the client walkthrough. They are asking: "Do you offer detailed cleanup services specifically for post-construction projects?"

Color Schema

The color scheme is designed to be professional, trustworthy, and clean, with a bright accent to draw attention to key actions.

Color Hex Usage
Primary Blue #005A9C Headings, Hero/Footer Backgrounds, Main Branding
Accent Gold #FDB813 Call-to-Action Buttons, Links, Key Highlights
Dark Text #333333 Paragraphs, Body Content
Light Gray #f4f4f4 Section Backgrounds (e.g., Stats Bar)
