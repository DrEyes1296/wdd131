Our Commercial Cleaning Services

We offer a comprehensive suite of cleaning services designed to meet the unique needs of your business. Our teams are available Monday through Friday at any hour to ensure minimal disruption to your operations.

Commercial & Business Cleaning

Our core service provides reliable, routine cleaning to keep your workplace professional, sanitary, and productive. We create custom cleaning plans tailored to your facility's needs.

  • Restroom Sanitization
  • Trash & Recycling Removal
  • Vacuuming & Mopping
  • Surface Dusting & Wiping
  • Entryway & Lobby Cleaning

Floor Stripping & Waxing

Restore the shine and protect your investment in hard-surface flooring like VCT, vinyl, and linoleum. Our process removes old, worn wax and applies fresh coats for a durable, high-gloss finish.

  • Complete Removal of Old Finish
  • Deep Cleaning & Neutralization
  • Application of Professional-Grade Sealer
  • Multiple Coats of High-Quality Wax
  • High-Speed Buffing for Shine

Carpet Shampooing & Extraction

Our deep extraction methods remove embedded dirt, tough stains, and allergens from your commercial carpets, improving air quality and extending the life of your carpet.

  • Pre-Treatment for Stains & High-Traffic Areas
  • Hot Water Extraction Method
  • Removes Odors and Allergens
  • Fast Drying Times to Minimize Disruption
  • Regular Maintenance Plans Available

Post-Construction Cleanup

Construction projects leave a significant mess. Our specialized teams perform a meticulous, top-to-bottom cleaning to transform your construction site into a move-in-ready space.

  • Removal of Debris, Dust & Sawdust
  • Washing of All Surfaces
  • Cleaning of Interior Windows & Tracks
  • Floor Cleaning & Polishing
  • Final Touch-Up for Occupancy
